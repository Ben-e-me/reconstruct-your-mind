reconstruct your mind — story.txt authoring

The whole experience is written in one plain-text file: story.txt. A blank line starts a new beat. Below: every marker in monospace on the left, the resulting effect on the right. Values (timing, colors) are still experimental and live in the app's config.

Motion

appear

Words rise and fade in one after another (split-text), ~60ms apart.

page change

The whole beat blurs and fades out before the next appears (blur-text).

highlights

*gradient* words shimmer slowly through warm rust tones.

pause

Standalone … reveals dot-by-dot, slowly, as a moment to rest.
